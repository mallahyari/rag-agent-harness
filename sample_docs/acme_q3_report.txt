ACME Corporation — Q3 2025 Financial Report

Executive Summary
-----------------
ACME Corporation reported strong Q3 2025 results, with total revenue of $4.2 billion,
representing a 12% year-over-year increase. Net income reached $680 million, up 18%
from the prior year. Operating margin improved to 16.2%, driven by cost efficiencies
in the manufacturing division.

Revenue Breakdown
-----------------
Product revenue: $3.1 billion (+9% YoY)
Service revenue: $1.1 billion (+22% YoY)

The services segment outperformed expectations, driven by strong demand for ACME's
cloud analytics platform, which added 45,000 new enterprise customers in Q3.

Regional Performance
---------------------
North America: $2.4 billion (57% of total revenue)
Europe: $1.1 billion (26%)
Asia-Pacific: $0.7 billion (17%)

Asia-Pacific showed the highest growth rate at 31% YoY, primarily from expansion
in Japan and South Korea where ACME launched its flagship SmartGrid product line.

Guidance
--------
For Q4 2025, ACME expects revenue between $4.4 billion and $4.6 billion.
Full-year 2025 revenue guidance raised to $16.8 billion from $16.2 billion.
EPS guidance for full year 2025: $8.40–$8.60.

Key Risks
---------
- Supply chain disruptions in semiconductor components remain a concern.
- Currency headwinds from a stronger US dollar may impact international revenue.
- Regulatory scrutiny in the EU regarding ACME's data practices.
